Plans as IDC Rep

SHARED VISIONS
· Increase communication between FUHSD ASB and FUHSD class officers through monthly/bi-weekly meetings set during the summer.
· Promote and host easily accessible interschool events like fundraisers and beach clean-ups.
· Expand ASB events (not just major ones like dances) to accommodate for inter-school relations.
