“Who should I vote for?”

is the question that’s running through everyone’s minds. We all know to vote for the best candidate but what does that really mean.

All the candidates are committed. All the candidates are passionate. All the candidates will work their hardest to make your sophomore year the greatest it can be.

Then what does matter?

Committed doesn’t mean prepared. Passionate doesn’t mean extraordinary. Working hard doesn’t mean planning things out.

An officer who is EXPERIENCEd shows not only commitment but also skill and success to make the dedication worthwhile.
An officer who REACHES OUT shows not only genuine compassion but also the courage to do and inspire greatness.
An officer who brings IDEAS to the table shows not only the hard work he/she will put in but also deep understanding of the class’ needs.

Plans as Secretary

Communication
Memories require events. Events require teamwork. Teamwork requires communication. That is why I want to implement an efficient and reliable communication system with two parts: input and transparency, for next year.

Part I: YOUR Input
Polls
· mainly on Facebook, important ones through email (ex what type of fundraiser on FB)
· immediate and public feedback for officers

Signups and Notices
· signups for all class events: homecoming, rallies, Powderpuff, will be posted on FB (ex dance, decs, football coaching)
· notices for all open class meetings will be posted on FB (decs at Lilian’s house today at 4)

Participation in Class Events??
I understand that most of you do not have the time to be an actor or dancer for class events. But those who can’t are still as vital to our class as those who are, thus next year I hope to have the whole class participate in a scene, not just at the end during the class dance.

Transparency
Updates
· meeting minutes will be uploaded on the class website of every officer meeting (exception to secret, fun events for you guys)
· biweekly updates posted on the facebook group of what each of the officers is currently working on
· online budget of 2018: our expenditures, source of assets, and cash flow

Better use of the Website
· updates of class events (upcoming notices, summaries, etc.)
· PR of not only class events, but also Lynbrook and outside school opportunities
· anonymous forum (kind of like ask.fm but with comments)


Other Projects
Does a leader only try to keep everyone alive? No, a leader goes above and beyond.

· more fundraisers ! (there will be a sweet prize, if we reach our goal)
· class bonding (Great America on the weekends?)
· class recognition: did one of your friends do something amazing recently?
· class charity: impacting bigger than just Lynbrook
· PR class and global events: taking and posting pictures, mass posts, more freedom of the types of posts in the FB grou
